FAQ's

Question 1: What are your delivery options?
We offer same-day delivery for all orders placed before 12 PM. Additionally, we provide express delivery and standard shipping options.
Question 2: Can I customize the cakes?
Yes, we offer customization options for our cakes. You can choose the flavor, size, design, and message to personalize your cake.
Question 3: Do you have gluten-free options?
Yes, we have a variety of gluten-free cakes available. Please mention your dietary requirements while placing your order.
Question 4: What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery for your convenience.
Question 5: How can I track my order?
Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery.
Question 6: How do I return an item?
If you need to return an item, please contact our customer support team within 30 days of receiving your order. We will provide you with instructions on how to return the item and process your refund.
